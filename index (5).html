I currently study at The University of California Riverside studying Art studio. I have expirience enforcing my aristic talents through many different expiriences. I currently teach colorguard at a nearby middle school, I design shows and choreography for the students to perform in competions around southern california. Focusing on interior decorating and design, I have worked for a good part of my life in my family's buisness, designing kitchens and installing cabinets and countertops. I would say building and designing kitchens opened up my mind to interior design. I now study in Art Studio furthering my education in stylistic hormony and using this knowlege towards interior design and decorating.

In my own time I practice interior design and decoration on my own, with the use of family's homes and my own space. Doing so has provided me with a great hands on learning expirience, which has taught so much in developing my skills. One of my favorite parts of designing these spaces is learning how to do it at a reasonable price. I understand that hiring an interiro designer or decorator would be very intimedating, money wise, for anyone of a lower class. I want to re-shape this fear and make my services available to all because I truly beleive everybody should feel at home and comfortable in their own space without having to break the bank.

Some things I do to make my projects cost efficient are upcycling, restoration, and essentially DIY. I find that upcylcing older items, wheather that's by reupholstering a chair, Re-finishing the wood of an older dressor, and taking anything with potential and making it into something functional and decorative for a space. Using these methods help keep costs low and make it easier to create unique pieces suitable for ones likling. I enjoy this process deeply as it allows me to excersize my artisic skills by designing individual pieces and connects me more personally to the project.
